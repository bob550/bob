At last doubt grew into suspicion, and suspicion into fierce terror. Had Marian been
abducted by Monceux, and did the Squire fear to tell him?
On the night before the Fair he took courage and marched up to the castle entrance, then
wound his horn for the bridge to be lowered. Now, if Monceux could but have known,
Robin would have been easy prey.
He rushed across the bridge soon as it had fallen, clangingly, upon the buttresses. The
same old servant met him at the gates, holding it open just a little way so that he might
peer forth. Robin pulled his cloak about himself.
"I would see Master Montfichet, and at once," he began.

questioning?"
"I am Robin Hood," said Robin, in desperation, "and now, for the love of Heaven, give

"It was upon the morning of that day," the man replied; "and I promise you, we have not

Robin turned abruptly from him. Next minute he was running blindly under the night
towards the city gates.
