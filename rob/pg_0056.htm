Soon afterward rain fell and a heavy storm raged amongst the trees. The two youths crept
into the hollow of one of the larger oaks to shelter themselves. Whilst waiting there they
heard the noise of an approaching cavalcade. It was a body of archers coming from
Lincoln to compete for the purse of gold.
They cantered past the tree wherein Robin and Stuteley lay hidden, and took no heed of
the drenching rain. All were merry with wine and very confident that one amongst them
would surely win the prize. The only question was, Which one?
